Counselling Services

I offer in-person and virtual sessions for individuals at every stage of adult life. Each session is shaped around what matters to you most.

Young Adult Counselling

Your twenties and thirties come loaded with pressure — career decisions, relationship uncertainty, the anxiety of constantly measuring yourself against where you think you should be. Social media doesn't help. Neither does the feeling that everyone else has it figured out.

I provide a space to work through the overwhelm and develop practical tools for moving forward. Together, we'll identify what's holding you back so you can build self-trust, tap into your agency, and become a more confident, grounded version of yourself.

Common areas of focus: career and academic stress, dating and relationships, social anxiety, decision paralysis, self-doubt, identity exploration, boundary setting.

Adult Counselling

Life transitions can knock you sideways — the loss of a relationship, feeling disconnected from who you used to be, the weight of expectations, or that quiet midlife question: How do I want to show up for the second half?

With warmth and a practical approach, I'll support you to get clarity on your needs, reassess your values and priorities, and begin making deliberate, meaningful choices about the life you're still building — rather than the one you think you're supposed to have.

Common areas of focus: life transitions, divorce, grief, burnout, midlife reassessment, anxiety, chronic illness, involuntary childlessness, relationship patterns, self-worth.

Older Adult Counselling

Aging brings perspective — and it can also bring challenges you didn't see coming. Grief, loneliness, health concerns, loss of identity, questions about your place in the world. These are real, and they deserve real attention.

I offer a compassionate space to process these shifts, manage the weight they carry, and build the inner resources you need to navigate this chapter with strength, meaning, and renewed purpose.

Common areas of focus: grief and loss, isolation, health challenges, caregiver burnout, retirement adjustment, legacy and purpose, anxiety, pain management.
