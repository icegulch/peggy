Hi, I'm Peggy

Registered Clinical Counsellor in Vancouver, BC

I'm a Registered Clinical Counsellor who values authentic, meaningful dialogue. I bring warmth, curiosity, and honesty into the room — building a space where you can be yourself without pretense or performance.

I work with capable, reflective people who are often doing everything "right" on paper — yet still feel overwhelmed, disconnected, or weighed down by anxiety, grief, or self-doubt. Whether you're facing something new or working through longstanding patterns, we'll look at what's actually keeping you stuck and develop practical ways to shift it.

Together, we'll slow down and explore:

  • • The patterns you repeat and where they come from
  • • The needs, emotions, and fears driving your responses
  • • The parts of you seeking more ease, clarity, and self-trust
  • • The stories you've carried — and the ones you're ready to rewrite

Counselling is my second career. After years of feeling unfulfilled, I made the leap in my thirties to train as a therapist. I hold a Master's in Counselling Psychology and have worked with leading agencies, health authorities, non-profits, and private clinics. I also have lived experience with loss, unrealized dreams, and chronic illness — I know what it takes to build more inner calm and openness, and to live and relate with greater ease.

I also know therapy doesn't need to feel heavy the whole time. Sometimes we laugh. Humor can be a vital part of healing.

My Approach

You're done with therapy that just feels like venting.

You've been in sessions before where the therapist nods along but never pushes back. Who validates without helping you see the blind spots. You want something more.

You want a therapist who respects your intelligence and agency — but who isn't afraid to point out when you're working against yourself. Someone who will truly engage with you, not just sit back and listen.

With warmth, empathy, and sometimes humor, I'll hold you accountable to yourself and work collaboratively with you to address what you're facing. This may mean reconnecting with forgotten strengths, making sense of patterns that no longer serve you, or finding new ways of relating to yourself and others.

I'll help you learn to trust yourself and tap into your own agency — moving beyond insight and into meaningful action. Together, we'll align your energy with your values so you can do less of what depletes you and more of what energizes you.

Therapeutic Modalities

I tailor each session to your individual needs, drawing from a range of evidence-based approaches.

Acceptance & Commitment Therapy (ACT)

Developing psychological flexibility through acceptance of difficult thoughts and feelings, clarifying your values, and committing to meaningful action.

Internal Family Systems (IFS)

Exploring your internal dynamics and conflicts, identifying and healing different parts of yourself to achieve greater harmony and integration.

EMDR Therapy

Using eye movements to address emotional responses attached to memories, reducing their charge and facilitating the reprocessing of difficult experiences.

Cognitive Behavioural Therapy (CBT)

Addressing cognitive distortions and behaviour patterns, challenging unhelpful thoughts, and building healthier coping strategies.

Psychodynamic Therapy

Exploring unconscious patterns and past experiences to understand how early relationships and unresolved conflicts shape your present thoughts and behaviours.

Narrative Therapy

Exploring your personal stories to find new perspectives, rewrite limiting narratives, and reconnect with your strengths and agency.

Emotionally Focused Therapy (EFT)

Identifying and reshaping emotional responses and attachment patterns to create secure bonds and healthier ways of connecting.

Solution-Focused Therapy

Identifying your strengths and past successes, envisioning your preferred future, and developing practical strategies for achieving your goals.
