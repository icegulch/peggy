Turn Insight Into Action and Build the Life You Want.

Many people understand themselves—and still feel stuck. You may know why you think and feel the way you do, and still fall into the same patterns when stress hits.

Therapy is where understanding becomes change. Rather than managing symptoms or willing yourself to think positively, we slow down and look directly at what keeps pulling you back—and we practice responding differently.

This work isn't about quick relief. It's about changing how you relate to your thoughts, emotions, and choices so that something genuinely different becomes possible. Less struggle. More agency. A life you're actively building, not just getting through.

Does this sound familiar?

• You often feel stressed, overwhelmed or anxious
• You overthink everything and often feel paralyzed.
• You're navigating grief, loss, or a life transition that's left you unmoored.
• You long for deeper connection in your relationships but don't know how to get there.
• You keep saying yes to everyone else and running on empty.
• Your inner critic runs the show, and the old protective habits aren't serving you anymore.
• You're struggling to move on from a past or present hurt.

You have the power to decide how you want to move forward with your life.
You might just need a little help—and that's exactly what I do.

Young Adult Counselling

Your twenties and thirties come with relentless decision-making — career pressure, dating and relationships, identity questions. I help you cut through the overwhelm, build self-trust, and move forward with confidence and clarity.

Adult Counselling

While midlife brings understanding and experience, there's often a restless call for something more. Stress, the weight of expectations and the question of “Is this it?” can shake your sense of direction. I help you clarify what matters, realign with your values, and make thoughtful deliberate choices for the second half of life.

Older Adult Counselling

Aging brings wisdom — and unexpected challenges. Grief, loneliness, health concerns, questions of legacy. I offer a steady and compassionate space to process these shifts, find meaning and move forward with purpose and strength.

Still deciding? Book a free 20-minute consultation with me to see if we're a fit.

Meet your counsellor, Peggy Harowitz

I believe that honest, meaningful conversations are the precursor to change. As a counsellor in Vancouver with over 8 years' experience, I support individuals through life's challenges. I hold a Master's in Counselling and am a registered clinical counsellor (RCC) in good standing with the BC Association of Clinical Counsellors (BCACC). My approach is warm, straightforward, and grounded in evidence-based methods tailored to your needs. I'm here to help you navigate through life's inevitable ups and downs so you can find more choice, connection and vitality within yourself and your relationships. You can expect honesty, compassion and even humour. Therapy doesn't have to be clinical and heavy to be effective.

Guiding Principles

I provide down-to-earth, compassionate support for adults navigating life's difficulties. Whether you're feeling stuck, disconnected, or weighted down by anxiety, grief, or self doubt, I'm here for you. Together, we'll tailor each session to your needs using evidence-based approaches.

Painful experiences can create internal turmoil — part of you wants to change and another isn't sure how. Part of you wants to connect and another wants to withdraw. This can be exhausting. My role is to help you reduce the inner tension, and access the healthy self within, so you can live with greater ease and in alignment with your values.
